PushUpGo Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-17 · Version 1.0

Overview

PushUpGo is a local‑first push‑up tracker and visualization app. We do not require account sign‑in and do not integrate any third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs. Your data is stored on‑device by default. You may optionally enable iCloud (CloudKit) sync, which is encrypted and handled within your Apple iCloud account.

Data We Collect and Store

  • Workout Records: date, count, optional note, source (e.g., manual/quick). Model: PushUpRecord.
  • Subscription Status: whether Pro, last confirmed product ID, optional expiry date, last updated time. Model: SubscriptionStatus.
  • Widget Snapshot (for widgets): aggregated numbers for the last 30 days, totals, current streak. Stored in App Group UserDefaults.

This data is generated by you in the app and used to provide core features (stats, trends, heatmap, CSV export, widget display).

How We Use Data

  • Display your statistics, trends, and heatmaps inside the app.
  • Create widget snapshots containing aggregated numbers only.
  • Unlock Pro features based on subscription status (e.g., iCloud sync, export).

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or behavioral profiling.

Storage and Sync

  • On‑device: Records and subscription status are stored locally using SwiftData.
  • iCloud (optional): When enabled in Settings, data is synced via CloudKit to your iCloud account. We operate no own servers and cannot access your iCloud data.
  • Widget sharing: Snapshots are shared between the app and the widget via App Group.

In‑App Purchases

Purchases and restorations are processed by Apple’s StoreKit. Transaction handling and verification are performed by Apple. We store only the minimal subscription status locally to provide features accordingly.

Export and Deletion

  • You can export a CSV file in Settings and save/share it yourself.
  • You may delete records in the app; if iCloud sync is enabled, deletions will sync across devices.
  • Uninstalling the app removes local data. If iCloud was enabled, manage remaining data in iCloud settings.

Children’s Privacy

PushUpGo is intended for general audiences and not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Local data is protected by iOS. iCloud sync uses Apple’s security mechanisms. Please secure your device and Apple ID.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will indicate the latest version and date in the app or on the relevant page.
