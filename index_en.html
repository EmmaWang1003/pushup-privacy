PushUpGo Privacy Policy

v1.1 · 2025-11-20
We respect your privacy. This document explains our data practices.

Introduction

PushUpGo is a local-first app. Your data is stored on your device and privately synced across your devices via iCloud (CloudKit). No login required.

Data We Collect

  • Push-up records: counts, date, optional notes.
  • App settings: language, theme, preferences.
  • Membership status: used only to unlock Pro features (e.g., Auto Count).
  • We do not collect: account info, contacts, location, device identifiers, push tokens.

How We Use Data

  • Statistics & trends: calendar heatmaps, weekly/monthly stats, trend analysis.
  • Personalized experience: preserve your display preferences and settings.
  • Membership unlock: determine access to Pro features; no behavioral profiling.

Storage & Sync

  • Local storage via SwiftData on your device.
  • Private sync via iCloud CloudKit between your devices.
  • Backup/restore managed by the system; you control iCloud settings.

Purchases & Membership

  • Pro features like Auto Count are exclusive to Pro members.
  • Purchases are processed by the App Store; we do not store payment data.
  • Membership status is used only for feature unlocks, not tied to identity.

Export

  • You can export your records (CSV) in Settings.

Children’s Privacy

We do not target children under 13, nor do we knowingly collect children’s data.

Security

  • We follow platform best practices, using system sandboxing and encrypted transport.
  • No cross-site tracking or third-party analytics.

Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share data with ad networks or data brokers.
  • We may comply with lawful requests when legally required.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you prominently in the app and update the version.
