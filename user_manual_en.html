PushUpGo User Manual

Last updated: 2025-11-17 · Version 1.0

Overview

PushUpGo is a local‑first push‑up tracker with quick logging, heatmaps, and trends. Your data is stored on‑device by default, with optional iCloud (CloudKit) sync. No sign‑in, no third‑party ads or analytics.

Getting Started

  • Quick Log: Tap preset buttons on Home (e.g., 10, 20, 30) to add counts.
  • Manual Entry: Enter a number in the input field and save.
  • Notes: Add optional notes to records to remember training context.

Records

  • Browse: View history by date and daily totals.
  • Edit: Use the inline editor to change counts or delete a record.
  • Bulk Delete: Select multiple records from the toolbar and confirm deletion.
  • Export: Go to Settings → Export Data (CSV) to generate and share a file.

Trends

  • Periods: Week, Month, Year, and All‑time.
  • Charts: Visualize changes over time to plan training.
  • Annual Heatmap: Landscape view for full‑year heat; portrait shows a rotation hint.

Monthly Calendar

  • Switch Month: Use arrows or the month picker.
  • Daily Totals: Tap a day to see the push‑up count.
  • Today Highlight: Today is outlined in blue for quick focus.

Settings & Share

  • iCloud Sync (Pro): Securely sync via CloudKit to your iCloud account.
  • Export CSV (Pro): Generate a .csv file and share or save it.
  • Rate & Share: Invite friends and rate the app on the App Store.
  • Manual & Privacy: View this user manual and the privacy policy.

Widgets

Show today’s count, totals, and current streak on your Home Screen (aggregated snapshot via App Group).

Subscription

Handled by Apple StoreKit. We store minimal subscription status locally to unlock features.

Data & Privacy

  • On‑device storage via SwiftData.
  • Optional iCloud sync via CloudKit; we cannot access your iCloud data.
  • No third‑party ads or analytics; no selling or sharing of personal data.

FAQ

  • Language looks wrong: Adjust preferred languages in iOS Settings; the app adapts automatically.
  • No data visible: Pull to refresh or restart the app; ensure data was not cleared.
  • iCloud not syncing: Confirm iCloud is enabled and network is stable; try again later.
